Sheep (O):
	Advances the player. Moves randomly.

Villager (V):
	Does nothing. Doesn't move.

Win (W):
	When player is on its square, it gives a point to the player. Doesn't move.

Zombie (Z):
	Attacks when players are next to it. Instantly kills the player. Moves linearly.

Skeleton (S):
	Attacks at a range of 2 blocks normally, but when its special move is active, it can attack at a range of up to 3 blocks.
	Doesn't automatically kill the player, but detoriates its health with hits.
	
Sniffer (--O):
	Does nothing.
	
Creeper (C):
	Attacks at a range of 5 blocks every three moves. Moves linearly
